Cookies burnt on the edges but raw in the middle? 🔥🍪 Your oven’s temp sensor may be toast! This tiny probe tells the control board when to cycle heat—when it fails, you get uneven cooking or never reach the set temp. Grab a multimeter and look for about 1,080 Ω at room temp to know for sure. Replace a $20 sensor and skip a $200 service call! Save this reel, tag a friend who’s sick of half-baked dinners, and bring your bakes back to perfection. 🛠️✨
